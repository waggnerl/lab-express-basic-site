Early Career

Ronaldo's professional career began in 2002, when he signed with Sporting CP in Portugal. He quickly established himself as one of the most talented young players in the country, catching the attention of several top clubs, including Manchester United.

Breakthrough at Manchester United

In 2003, Ronaldo signed with Manchester United, where he became a key player for the club. He helped the team win multiple titles, including three Premier League titles and a Champions League trophy. During his time at the club, he developed into one of the best attacking players in the world, earning accolades and recognition for his performances.

Success at Real Madrid

In 2009, Ronaldo transferred to Real Madrid, where he continued his success. He helped the team win four Champions League titles and two La Liga titles, establishing himself as one of the greatest players in the history of the club. He became the all-time top scorer for Real Madrid, breaking several records along the way.

Move to Juventus

In 2018, Ronaldo signed with Juventus, where he continued his success in Italy. He won two Serie A titles in two seasons with the club, showcasing his ability to succeed at the highest level in multiple countries.

International Career

Ronaldo has also had a successful international career, representing Portugal in multiple European Championships and World Cups. He helped lead Portugal to victory in the 2016 European Championships, scoring the winning goal in the final. He has been a crucial player for the national team for many years, earning recognition and praise for his performances.

Important Moments

Throughout his career, Ronaldo has been a part of many important moments and games. He has scored crucial goals in high-stakes matches, earning him the reputation of being a big-game player. Some of his most memorable moments include scoring his 100th goal for Real Madrid, helping Portugal win the European Championships, and scoring his 700th career goal.

Awards and Accomplishments

Ronaldo has won numerous awards and accolades throughout his career. He has won five Ballon d'Or awards, given to the best player in the world, and has been named to the UEFA Team of the Year multiple times. He is also the all-time top scorer for both Real Madrid and the UEFA Champions League.
